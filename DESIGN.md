## Overview
ReadMaster should feel like a premium Korean academy lobby: calm, literate, warm, and credible. The hero uses the actual reception image as the main first-viewport signal, with text placed on a softened overlay so the brand scene remains visible and readable.

## Colors
Use cream paper, dark ink, forest green, and brass. Avoid loud gradients or colorful decoration.

## Typography
Use serif display type only for major headings. Keep body copy compact and readable for parents scanning on mobile.

## Layout
The hero image is a full background, not a separate card. Place copy on the left where the image has darker empty space; keep the logo wall visible on the center/right.

## Components
Buttons remain brass or outlined. Cards use 8px radius and restrained borders.

## Do's and Don'ts
Do preserve the academy image as the main visual anchor. Do not cover the wall logo with floating panels.
